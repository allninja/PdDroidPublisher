PPP

What is it about ?

PPP stands for PdPartyPublisher and it's a project that will help you to export your own Pure-Data patches thanks to libpd to a full fledged application, that you can distribute as you want - in respect of the license agreement. It's not all but that is what you can do at minima, at most you can build custom guis and benefit from a standard midi clock shared over wifi for all your patches (that's last one is the "party" part !)

PPP is based on PdDroidParty by Chris McCormick, itself based on Libpd by Peter Brinkman, itself based on Pure-Data by Miller Puckette.

Our goal was not replace anything, but to make it possible for you to integrate new stuff into your workflow and let also non-pders to benefit from your voodoo pd skills in their standard midi setup.

You can check out the very first batch of applications (still alpha) at this page

So from now either you are :

  • an app user : and you can check the documentation here to start jamming.
  • a pd developper : and you can check the documentation here to start building an application from a Pure-Data patch.
  • a software developper : and you can check the documentation on the repo's wiki to start contributing.
